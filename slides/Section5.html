General Comments

  • Hierarchical modeling techniques can be used to tackle incredibly complicated problems.
  • Fancy models should not take the place of careful model selection efforts.
  • Consider studying applied Bayesian statistics if you have time and want to dramatically epand your analytics toolkit.

Conclusions

Ebola 2014

  • Making firm predictions in such an uncertain situation is difficult.
  • There is no guarantee that the chosen spline basis will extrapolate well, or firm estimates of the reliability of the data used.
  • Nevertheless, preliminary evidence indicates that, should the situation continue to progress as it has, we may hope for a conclusion some time in the spring.
  • This prediction does not incorporate the likelihood of further spread in a place like Mali, or many other potential setbacks.
